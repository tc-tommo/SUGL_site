About SUGL

The Scottish University Gymnastics League (SUGL) is the premier organization for university gymnastics in Scotland. We bring together students from universities across the country to compete, train, and celebrate the sport of gymnastics.

Our mission is to promote gymnastics at the university level, provide competitive opportunities, and foster a community of passionate athletes and supporters.
