Training Weekend

Two-day weekend of training and fun

October 4-5, 2025
Stirling

Event Overview

Join us for a relaxed weekend of training and socialising, get to know other universities and make new friends!

Hosting University

Stirling University

Accommodation

Local students can arrange to be a host or arriving participants can be paired with a host if they wish to have a free accomodation option. This is not compulsory, but in previous years it has been a great way to make new friends and support the community.

What to Bring

  • Gymnastics attire (leotard, shorts, t-shirt)
  • Gymnastics shoes or bare feet
  • Water bottle
  • Lunch and snacks
  • Change of clothes
  • Personal equipment (grips, wristbands if needed)
